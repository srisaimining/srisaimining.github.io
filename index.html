About INDCORE Mining SARL

Established on 18th November 2023, INDCORE Mining SARL is headquartered in Abidjan, Ivory Coast. Our company collaborates with a leading Indian mining firm with over three decades of coal mining expertise.

  • Specializing in manganese mining near Yamoussoukro, Ivory Coast.
  • Committed to environmentally sustainable mining practices.
  • Leveraging decades of mining expertise from our Indian partners to drive growth in Ivory Coast's mineral sector.

INDCORE Mining SARL is excited about the opportunities in Ivory Coast and aims to contribute to the country's economic growth by sustainably exploiting its rich mineral resources. Our proactive approach ensures the development of long-term partnerships and value creation in the region.
